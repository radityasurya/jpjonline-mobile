# Privacy Policy for JPJOnline

**Last Updated:** December 3, 2025

## Introduction

JPJOnline ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application for Malaysian driving license preparation.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you register for an account, we collect:

- **Name**: To personalize your experience
- **Email Address**: For account authentication and communication
- **Password**: Stored securely using industry-standard encryption

### Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use the app:

- **Learning Progress**: Notes viewed, exams taken, scores achieved
- **Bookmarks**: Materials you've saved for later reference
- **Activity History**: Your interaction with learning materials
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **App Performance Data**: Crash reports and error logs (when enabled)

### Data Storage

All personal data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms:

- **Local Storage**: AsyncStorage for offline functionality
- **No Cloud Sync**: Your data remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to sync

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- **Provide Services**: Enable core app functionality including learning materials and practice tests
- **Track Progress**: Monitor your learning journey and exam performance
- **Personalize Experience**: Customize content based on your learning patterns
- **Improve App**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix bugs
- **Communicate**: Send important updates about the app (if you opt-in)

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

- **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly authorize us to share information
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law, court order, or government regulation
- **Service Providers**: With trusted third-party services that help us operate the app (e.g., analytics, crash reporting) - only when you enable these features
- **Business Transfers**: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption**: Passwords and sensitive data are encrypted
- **Secure Storage**: Local data is stored using platform-specific secure storage
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to personal information
- **Regular Updates**: Security patches and updates to protect against vulnerabilities

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### Access and Portability

- View all personal data we have about you
- Export your learning progress and exam history

### Correction

- Update your profile information at any time
- Correct inaccurate data

### Deletion

- Delete your account and all associated data
- Remove specific bookmarks or activity history
- Clear local storage through app settings

### Opt-Out

- Disable analytics and crash reporting
- Opt out of promotional communications
- Control which features collect data

To exercise these rights, access the Profile section in the app or contact us at <privacy@jpjonline.com>.

## Children's Privacy

JPJOnline is intended for users aged 16 and above, as this is the minimum age for obtaining a driving license in Malaysia. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 16, please contact us immediately.

## Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

### Analytics Services (Optional)

When enabled, we use:

- **Crash Reporting**: To identify and fix app crashes
- **Usage Analytics**: To understand how features are used

You can disable these services in the app settings.

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as:

- Your account is active
- Necessary to provide services
- Required by law

When you delete your account:

- All personal data is permanently removed from our systems
- Local data is cleared from your device
- Backups are purged within 30 days

## International Data Transfers

Your information is primarily stored locally on your device. If we process data on servers, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Sending an in-app notification for significant changes

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
